  • Character Overview

    Character Overview Cinnamoroll, with the English name Cinnamoroll, is a popular cartoon character launched by Japan's Sanrio Company in 2002. It is positioned as a cute and healing puppy, mainly targeting children, teenagers, and young groups who love cute culture, especially female consumers.

  • Character Traits

    Analysis of Cinnamoroll's Character Traits In the star-studded character map of the Sanrio family, Cinnamoroll is like a gentle cream-colored moonlight, with fluffy healing power and cloud-like dreaminess. Since its birth in 2001, it has quickly become popular worldwide. This white puppy inspired by cinnamon rolls not only captivates hearts with its fluffy and soft appearance but also builds a three-dimensional and vivid character image with its contrasting cute personality traits.
